About Me

Dr. Daniel (Xunzhu) Tang is a PostDoc researcher at the TruX Group, Interdisciplinary Security and Trust Centre (SnT), University of Luxembourg. His research focuses on advancing the intersection of Software Engineering and Artificial Intelligence, with particular emphasis on Code Generation, LLM-based Agents, and Automatic Program Repair.

His work has been published in top-tier venues across NLP, SE, and ML communities, including ICSE, ASE, EMSE, TOSEM, ICLR, ACL, NAACL, and EMNLP. Dr. Tang actively contributes to the research community as a PC member and reviewer for prestigious conferences such as SOSP, ACL, EMNLP, and MSR.

Research Interests

  • LLM-Based Agents for Software Engineering
  • Code Generation and Program Synthesis
  • Automatic Program Repair
  • Software Security and LLM Security
  • Patch Representation and Explanation
  • Large Language Models for Code
